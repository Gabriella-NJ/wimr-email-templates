Screening for COVID 19

For the safety of staff and patients, we are now asking all visitors to the Woolcock Clinic including patients, parents, or carers to perform a COVID test ahead of their sleep study. This could be:

  • A PCR test done within 48 hours
  • A rapid antigen test performed at the Woolcock Clinic. Kits can be provided at the cost of $12.00 or you can bring your own. The test must be performed under the supervision of our technicians

Please do not attend your appointment if:

  • You have been diagnosed with COVID-19
  • You have had any of the following symptomsâ€‹ in the last 14 days:  
    • fever (37.5° or higher)

  • You have developed any other respiratory symptoms
  • You have been a contact of someone diagnosed with COVID-19

Payment of your sleep studies and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a facility fee of $460.00 to be paid for your CPAP Review and Daytime sleep studies. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Pensioner or Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a facility fee of $120.00 to be paid for your stay. You will need to present your Medicare card and any other cards and make payment when you check in at Reception. Please note that if you are a research participant, there will be no facility fee payment required.
  • Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $200.00.
