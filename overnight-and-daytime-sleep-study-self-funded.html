Screening for COVID 19

For the safety of staff and patients, we are now asking all visitors including patient, parents, or carer to The Woolcock to provide evidence of a negative COVID test when they attend for their sleep study. Please arrange for a COVID swab no more than 72 hours before your attendance and present your negative result to reception when you arrive. If you are having difficulty arranging your test, or have any questions, please reply to this email and our friendly bookings team will be in touch.

Payment of your sleep studies and cancelling or changing your appointment

As a self-funded patient, you are required to pay the full costs for your overnight study and day study. We will ask you to pay this when you arrive.  We encourage you to speak with your insurer prior to the sleep study to find out what they cover and how much you can claim back.

The cost of your sleep study:

 

  1. 12254 or 12258 - Adult Diagnostic Sleep Study = $970.00 and
  2. Facility fee of $460.00 – Unfortunately this fee does not have an item code and therefore cannot be claimed back.  

 

 

Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing.  If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $200.00.
