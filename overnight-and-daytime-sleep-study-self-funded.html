Screening for COVID 19

Please do not attend your appointment if:

  • You have been diagnosed with COVID-19
  • You have had any of the following symptoms in the last 7 days:
    • fever (37.5° or higher)
    • cough
    • sore throat
    • shortness of breath (difficulty breathing)
    • runny nose
    • loss of taste
    • loss of smell.
  • You have developed any other respiratory symptoms
  • You are a close contact of someone diagnosed with COVID-19

If you answer yes to any of the above questions, please reschedule your appointment to at least 7 days after symptoms resolve or after your last contact with a COVID-19 positive person.

You will be asked to fill out a COVID-19 screening questionnaire on your arrival. If possible COVID-19 exposure is suspected, you will undergo a RAT test.

You can either supply your own RAT kit or purchase one from the Woolcock for the cost of $12. The test will be carried out under the supervision of one of our Sleep Technicians.

If you test positive at the Woolcock Clinic, you will be asked to return home immediately and follow NSW Health guidelines. The appointment will be rescheduled for a later date.

All patients attending the Woolcock Clinic are required to wear a mask.

Payment of your sleep studies and cancelling or changing your appointment

As a self-funded patient, you are required to pay the full costs for your overnight study and day study. We will ask you to pay this when you arrive.  We encourage you to speak with your insurer prior to the sleep study to find out what they cover and how much you can claim back.

The cost of your sleep study:

  1. 12254 or 12258 - Adult Diagnostic Sleep Study = $970.00 and
  2. Facility fee of $520.00 – Unfortunately this fee does not have an item code and therefore cannot be claimed back.  

Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing.  If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $200.00.
