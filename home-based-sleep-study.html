Screening for COVID 19

For the safety of staff and patients, we are now asking all visitors including patient, parents, or carer to The Woolcock to provide evidence of a negative COVID test when they attend for their sleep study. Please arrange for a COVID swab no more than 72 hours before your attendance and present your negative result to reception when you arrive. If you are having difficulty arranging your test, or have any questions, please reply to this email and our friendly bookings team will be in touch.

Payment of your sleep study and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a fee of $150.00 to be paid for your sleep study. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Pensioner or Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a fee of $60.00 to be paid for your overnight stay. You will need to present your Medicare card and any other cards and make payment when you check in at Reception.
  • Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $100.00.
