Screening for COVID 19

Please do not attend your appointment if:

  • You have been diagnosed with COVID-19
  • You have had any of the following symptomsâ€‹ in the last 14 days:  
    • fever (37.5° or higher)
    • cough

    • shortness of breath (difficulty breathing)
    • runny nose

  • You have developed any other respiratory symptoms

If you answer yes to any of the above questions please reschedule your appointment to at least 14 days after symptoms, travel or hotspot attendance or book a phone consultation with one of our specialists. If you develop acute respiratory illness or fever please call 1800 022 222, or call your local hospital or GP for further advice.

Patients who attend the Woolcock for a sleep study or face-to-face CPAP review will be asked to fill out a COVID-19 screening questionnaire. If possible COVID-19 exposure is suspected, the patient will undergo a RAT test.

You can either supply your own RAT kit or purchase one from the Woolcock for the cost of $12. The test will be carried out under the supervision of one of our Sleep Technicians or CPAP Therapists.

If you test positive at the Woolcock Clinic, you will be asked to return home immediately and following NSW Health guidelines. The appointment will be rescheduled for a later date.

All patients attending the Woolcock Clininc are required to wear a mask.

Payment of your sleep study and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a fee of $150.00 to be paid for your sleep study. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Pensioner or Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a fee of $60.00 to be paid for your overnight stay. You will need to present your Medicare card and any other cards and make payment when you check in at Reception.
  • Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $100.00.
