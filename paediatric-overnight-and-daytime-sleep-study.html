Screening for COVID 19
For the safety of staff and patients, we are now asking all visitors including patient, parents, or carer to The Woolcock to provide evidence of a negative COVID test when they attend for their sleep study. Please arrange for a COVID swab no more than 72 hours before your attendance and present your negative result to reception when you arrive. If you are having difficulty arranging your test, or have any questions, please reply to this email and our friendly bookings team will be in touch.
Please do not attend your appointment if:
• You have been diagnosed with COVID-19
• You have had any of the following symptoms in the last 14 days:
o fever (37.5° or higher)
o cough
o sore throat
o shortness of breath (difficulty breathing)
o runny nose
o loss of taste
o loss of smell.
• You have developed any other respiratory symptoms
• You have been a contact of someone diagnosed with COVID-19

Payment of your sleep studies and cancelling or changing your appointment
• Payment: There is a facility fee of $510.00 to be paid for your child’s overnight and daytime sleep studies. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a facility fee of $120.00 to be paid for the sleep studies. You will need to present your Medicare card and any other cards and make payment when you check in at Reception.
• Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change the sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $200.00.
If you have any queries or require additional information, please do not hesitate in contacting us on 02 9114 0000.
