Payment of your sleep study

 As a self-funded patient, you are required to pay the full costs for your overnight study and CPAP consultation. We will ask you to pay this when you arrive.  We encourage you to speak with your insurer prior to the sleep study to find out what they cover and how much you can claim back.

The cost of your sleep study:

The total cost of your sleep study is $1,010.00. This fee is due in full upon arrival for your sleep study. It is comprised of the following items:

  1. CPAP Review Study - $585.00 (Medicare Benefit Scheme Item Number 12205)
  2. Facility Fee - $350.00 (No MBS Item Number)
  3. CPAP Consultation- $75 (No MBS Item Number)

Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing.  If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $100.00.
