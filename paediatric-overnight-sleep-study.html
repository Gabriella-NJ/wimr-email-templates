Screening for COVID 19

For the safety of staff and patients, we are now asking all visitors to the Woolcock Clinic including patients, parents, or carers to perform a COVID test ahead of their sleep study. This could be:

  • A PCR test done within 48 hours
  • A rapid antigen test performed at the Woolcock Clinic. Kits can be provided at the cost of $12.00 or you can bring your own. The test must be performed under the supervision of our technicians

Please do not attend your appointment if:

  • You have been diagnosed with COVID-19
  • You have had any of the following symptomsâ€‹ in the last 14 days:  
    • fever (37.5° or higher)
    • cough
    • sore throat
    • shortness of breath (difficulty breathing)
    • runny nose
    • loss of taste
    • loss of smell.
  • You have developed any other respiratory symptoms
  • You have been a contact of someone diagnosed with COVID-19

If you answer yes to any of the above questions please reschedule your appointment to at least 14 days after symptoms, travel or hotspot attendance or book a phone consultation with one of our specialists. If you develop acute respiratory illness or fever please call 1800 022 222, or call your local hospital or GP for further advice.

Payment of your overnight sleep study and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a facility fee of $320.00 to be paid for the sleep study. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a facility fee of $60.00 to be paid for the sleep study. You will need to present your Medicare card and any other cards and make payment when you check in at Reception.
  • Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change the sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $100.00.

If someone will be picking you and your child up in the morning after the sleep study and if they are unable to enter the building, please ask them to call 02 9114 0266 and a staff member will be able to let them in.

If you have any queries or require additional information, please do not hesitate in contacting us on 02 9114 0000.
