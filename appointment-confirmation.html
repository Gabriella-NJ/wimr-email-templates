Before your appointment

  1. The attached Patient Registration Form and Consent Form needs to be completed and returned to reception@woolcock.org.au prior to your appointment.
  2. Please email your referral to reception@woolcock.org.au prior to your appointment. Note:
  • A specialist referral is valid for 3 months and a GP referral is valid for 12 months. Without a valid referral at the time of your consultation/treatment, Medicare will not be able to process your claim.
  • Approval documentation from your Insurer needs to be provided in the case of a Workers’ Compensation or Third-Party Claim. If not supplied, payment will need to be made on the day by the patient.
  1. You will receive a confirmation SMS 96 hours before your appointment. To secure your appointment, you must SMS ‘Yes” to confirm your attendance.

 

What to bring:

  1. Medicare card/Pensioner card/Concession card/DVA card/Centrelink Health Care card
  2. Test results (for example, blood tests, X-rays, scans)
  3. Medication list
