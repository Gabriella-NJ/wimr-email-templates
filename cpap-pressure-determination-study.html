Payment of your overnight sleep study and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a facility fee of $270.00 to be paid for your overnight stay. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Pensioner or Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a facility fee of $60.00 to be paid for your overnight stay. You will need to present your Medicare card and any other cards and make payment when you check in at Reception. Please note that if you are a research participant, there will be no facility fee payment required.
  • Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change your study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $100.00.
